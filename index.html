Our Transparent Framework

Every tool is evaluated across 5 clear criteria grounded in concrete factors. No hidden agenda‚Äîjust honest assessment.

üìä

Data Collection

Criteria: Does it ask for unnecessary personal info? Can you use it anonymously? What fields are required?

üîê

Data Protection

Criteria: Is data encrypted in transit? Do they have security certifications? Track record of breaches?

üì±

Third-Party Sharing

Criteria: Do they sell data to advertisers? Are there hidden trackers? Aggressive ad networks?

‚úÖ

Legal Compliance

Criteria: COPPA compliance (under 13)? FERPA compliance (student records)? Account requirements?

üéì

Academic Integrity

Criteria: Designed to teach concepts? Or enable cheating? Does it encourage critical thinking?

‚úó HIGH RISK: Avoid if possible. Privacy violations, cheating facilitation, or security issues. Not recommended.

Tool Database

Transparent evaluations of popular AI tools and homework resources students use. Each rated on our 5-criterion framework.

Khanmigo

AI Tutor by Khan Academy

Low Risk

What it does: AI tutor using Socratic method (asks questions instead of giving answers).

Best for: Learning math, science, humanities with guided problem-solving.

Privacy: Built by trusted nonprofit. No ads. Student data protected. Requires parental consent for under 18.

Wolfram Alpha

Computational Knowledge Engine

Low Risk

What it does: Solves math and science problems with step-by-step explanations.

Best for: Understanding math methodology, not just getting answers.

Privacy: Clear privacy policy. Minimal data collection. Pro version removes ads. No unnecessary tracking.

Quizlet

Study Tool with AI

Low Risk

What it does: Flashcards, spaced repetition, and AI study partner for memorization.

Best for: Vocabulary, history, memorization-heavy subjects.

Privacy: Student-friendly. Free version available. Limited data collection. Privacy controls available.

ChatGPT

General AI Assistant

Medium Risk

What it does: Powerful AI for explaining concepts, writing assistance, brainstorming.

Best for: Research, concept explanation, brainstorming‚ÄîNOT for completing assignments.

Caution: Requires account. Data used for training. Can enable plagiarism if misused. Verify accuracy.

Photomath

Math Problem Solver

Medium Risk

What it does: Scan math problems, get step-by-step solutions via OCR.

Best for: Understanding methodology, checking work, algebra through calculus.

Caution: Easy to misuse as shortcut. Requires account. Ads in free version. Limited parental controls.

Brainly

Crowdsourced Q&A Platform

Medium Risk

What it does: Community of students answering homework questions + AI supplements.

Best for: Getting different perspectives, peer learning.

Caution: Quality varies. Crowdsourced answers may be inaccurate. Account required. Ads present.

Essay Mill Sites

Contract Cheating Services

High Risk

What it does: Services that write complete essays for you to submit as your own.

Why avoid: Direct academic dishonesty. Violates school policies.

Risks: School expulsion. Financial theft. Data harvesting. Identity theft. Zero learning value.

Unvetted Homework Sites

Unknown Homework Help Platforms

High Risk

What it does: Obscure websites with homework solutions and aggressive ads.

Why avoid: Privacy violations, malware risks, unreliable information.

Risks: Malware infections. Data breaches. Identity theft. Inaccurate information. Unsafe pop-ups.

Unregulated AI Tools

Unsecured or Sketchy AI Platforms

High Risk

What it does: Unknown AI platforms lacking transparency on data & security.

Why avoid: No accountability. No privacy protections.

Risks: Complete privacy violation. Unencrypted data. No security guarantees. Potential scams.
