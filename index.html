Are You Using the Right Tools Safely?

Students today use AI and online homework resources without fully understanding the privacy, security, and ethical risks. ClickWise is a student-led initiative that empowers you to make informed choices about the digital tools you use for learning.

We provide transparent risk assessments, practical guides, and community events to help you learn securely and responsibly.

Our Risk Assessment Framework

Every tool and resource is evaluated across five clear criteria to determine its safety and security level.

üìä Data Collection

What personal information does the platform ask for? Does it require more than necessary? Can you use it anonymously?

üîê Data Protection

How is your data encrypted? Does the site have a privacy policy? Are they transparent about security practices?

üì± Third-Party Sharing

Does the platform sell or share your data with advertisers? Are there unnecessary trackers or ads?

‚úÖ Compliance

Does it follow COPPA (Children's Online Privacy), FERPA, and other student protection laws?

üéì Academic Integrity

Is the tool designed to help learning, or to enable cheating? Does it teach concepts or just give answers?

Popular AI Tools & Homework Resources

Evaluated based on our framework. Click on any tool to learn more about its privacy practices and best uses.

Khanmigo

Khan Academy's AI Tutor

Low Risk

What it does: AI tutor that guides you through problems using Socratic method (asks questions instead of giving answers).

Good for: Concept learning, step-by-step problem solving, deep understanding.

Privacy: Built by trusted nonprofit. Student data is protected. No ads. Requires parent approval for under 18.

Wolfram Alpha

Computational Knowledge Engine

Low Risk

What it does: Solves math and science problems with step-by-step explanations, not just answers.

Good for: Math, physics, chemistry‚Äîunderstanding methodology.

Privacy: Clear privacy policy, minimal data collection. Pro version removes ads. No unnecessary tracking.

Quizlet Q-Chat

AI Study Partner

Low Risk

What it does: AI study buddy combined with millions of flashcard sets for memorization and concept testing.

Good for: Vocabulary, history, memorization-heavy subjects.

Privacy: Student-friendly. Free version available. Limited data collection. Privacy controls available.

ChatGPT

General-purpose AI Assistant

Medium Risk

What it does: Powerful AI that can explain concepts, write essays, debug code, and much more.

Good for: Brainstorming, concept explanation, research‚Äînot for completing assignments.

Concerns: Requires account creation (personal data). Uses your data for training. Doesn't verify accuracy. Can enable plagiarism if misused.

Photomath

Math Problem Solver

Medium Risk

What it does: Scan math problems, get step-by-step solutions via OCR technology.

Good for: Understanding methodology, checking work, algebra through early calculus.

Concerns: Easy to misuse as shortcut. Requires account. Shows ads in free version. Limited parental controls.

Brainly

Crowdsourced Q&A Platform

Medium Risk

What it does: Community of students and experts answering homework questions. AI supplements human answers.

Good for: Getting different perspectives, peer learning.

Concerns: Quality varies. Crowdsourced answers may be inaccurate. Account required. Ads in free version.

Essay Mill Sites

Pay-for-Essay Services

High Risk

What it does: Services that write complete essays for you to submit as your own.

Why you shouldn't use: Direct academic dishonesty. Violates school policies.

Risks: School expulsion. Financial theft. Data harvesting. No learning. Legal implications.

Unvetted Homework Sites

Unknown Online Homework Help

High Risk

What it does: Obscure websites offering homework solutions, often with aggressive ads and sketchy practices.

Why you shouldn't use: Privacy violations, malware risks, unreliable information.

Risks: Malware infections. Data breaches. Identity theft. Inaccurate information. Unsafe pop-ups.

Unregulated AI Tools

Unsecured or Sketchy AI Platforms

High Risk

What it does: Unknown AI platforms that lack transparency about data practices or security.

Why you shouldn't use: No accountability. No privacy protections.

Risks: Complete privacy violation. Unencrypted data. No security guarantees. Potential scams.
